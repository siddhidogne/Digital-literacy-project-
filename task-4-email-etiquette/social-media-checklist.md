# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

##  Do’s :
- Think before posting — ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your information.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Maintain a professional tone, especially when interacting with faculty or peers.
- Respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy discussions.

##  Don’ts :
- Do not share personal or sensitive information (like phone number, address, passwords).
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or trolling.
- Avoid spreading fake news or unverified information.
- Do not overshare details that may harm your reputation.
