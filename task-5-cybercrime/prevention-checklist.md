# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Do not share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone.
- Always verify unknown calls, messages, or links before responding.
- Avoid clicking on suspicious or unknown links.
- Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
- Do not use public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions.
- Keep your apps and devices updated regularly.

## UPI and Financial Safety Tips
- Never approve payment requests without verifying the sender.
- Remember that receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN.
- Do not scan unknown QR codes sent by strangers.
- Always check the receiver’s details before making a payment.

## Reporting Cybercrime
If you become a victim of cybercrime, report immediately:
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline Number: 1930
